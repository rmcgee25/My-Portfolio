About Me

Welcome to a space where technology meets purpose. My passion for building websites and apps comes from a place of wanting to make a difference. After years in social service work, I saw how communities—especially marginalized ones—were often overlooked by websites, apps, and software that had the power to address their needs. That’s why I’m learning to code: to create digital tools that heal, empower, and connect.

As someone who’s experienced and worked alongside those who’ve been underserved, I’m committed to building technology that speaks to us—meeting people where they are and offering what’s been missing. This is more than a portfolio; it’s a reflection of my belief in tech as a tool for positive change.

Well, that’s the hope! This portfolio is a snapshot of my journey toward that goal, showcasing a collection of beginner to intermediate projects created by me.
